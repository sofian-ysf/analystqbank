CFA Level I Exam: Frequently Asked Questions Summary
=====================================================

1. STUDY TIME COMMITMENT
-----------------------
Average Study Hours: Successful candidates report spending over 300 hours preparing for the Level I exam.
Preparation: Candidates should tailor their preparation to individual needs and circumstances.

2. EXAM FORMAT AND STRUCTURE
----------------------------
Total Questions: 180 multiple-choice questions.
Sessions: Divided into two sessions (sittings) of 135 minutes each.
    Questions per Session: Each session contains 90 questions.
Pacing: Advised to spend approximately 90 seconds per question.
Question Structure: Each item has a stem (question, statement, and/or table) and three unique answer choices (A, B, or C).
    Formats Used: 1. Sentence completion. 2. Direct questions.
